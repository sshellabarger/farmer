Everything You Need

Powerful features that stay out of your way.

Natural Conversations

No commands to memorize. Text naturally and FarmLink's AI understands what you need — pricing, inventory, orders, and more.

Priority Markets

Rank your best buyers. They see your listings first, with configurable notification delays before inventory goes wide.

Smart Dashboard

When you need more than text, the web dashboard shows everything — inventory, orders, analytics, and full message history.

Standing Orders

Markets can set up recurring orders. Same items, same schedule, zero effort. Just text 'standing order' to manage them.

Multi-Market Reach

List once, reach every market in your network. Or target specific buyers first. You control who sees what and when.

Instant Confirmations

Both sides get immediate confirmation of orders, changes, and pickups. No phone tag, no missed messages, no confusion.
