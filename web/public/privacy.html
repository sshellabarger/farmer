Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 24, 2026

FarmLink ("we," "our," or "us") operates the FarmLink platform, including the website at farmlink.us and our SMS-based messaging service (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our Service.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, phone number, email address, business name, and business location when you register for FarmLink.
  • Transaction data: Inventory listings, pricing, orders, delivery schedules, and other records generated through your use of the platform.
  • Message content: SMS and web chat messages exchanged through the FarmLink platform, which are processed by our AI assistant to facilitate transactions.
  • Usage data: Information about how you interact with the Service, including login times, features used, and device information.
  • Location information: Farm or market addresses you provide for delivery coordination.

2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the FarmLink platform and AI assistant
  • Facilitate communication between farms and markets
  • Process and manage orders, deliveries, and payments
  • Send transactional SMS messages including inventory notifications, order confirmations, delivery updates, and standing order reminders
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Generate analytics and reports for your account (visible only to you)
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Your mobile information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

3. SMS messaging and mobile privacy

By registering for FarmLink and providing your phone number, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to your use of the platform. These include inventory alerts, order confirmations, delivery notifications, and AI assistant responses to your text inquiries.

We do not send marketing or promotional SMS messages. All SMS communications are directly related to platform transactions and operations you initiate or participate in.

Message frequency varies based on your activity level. Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by texting STOP to our messaging number. See our Terms of Service for additional details on our messaging practices.

We do not share your mobile phone number or any information collected through SMS messaging with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

4. How we share your information

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Between farms and markets: When you list inventory, place orders, or arrange deliveries, relevant transaction details (product, quantity, price, business name, delivery address) are shared with the other party to the transaction.
  • Service providers: We work with third-party providers for SMS delivery (Telnyx), AI processing (Anthropic), hosting, and payment processing. These providers are contractually required to protect your information and use it only to provide services to us.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, subpoena, or government request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of FarmLink, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

5. Data retention

We retain your account information and transaction history for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Message history is retained for a rolling 12-month period for platform functionality and dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Data security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security assessments. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights and choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information through the FarmLink dashboard
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt out of SMS messaging at any time by texting STOP
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@farmlink.us.

8. Children's privacy

FarmLink is designed for use by businesses and individuals aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you via SMS or through the FarmLink dashboard at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
